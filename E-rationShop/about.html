About the Project

Digitising the Public Distribution System

E-Ration Shop connects ration card holders directly to their entitlements — transparently, and without middlemen.

Why this project exists

Millions of families depend on the Public Distribution System (PDS) for subsidised food grains every month. Long queues, opaque stock records, and delayed grievance redressal have long been pain points for cardholders and shopkeepers alike.

E-Ration Shop is a frontend prototype that reimagines the ration counter as a digital service — where a family can check their entitlement, place an order, pay online, and track a complaint, all from a phone or a shared kiosk.

Project Goals
  • Reduce queue time at Fair Price Shops.
  • Give every cardholder visibility into their monthly stock.
  • Provide a transparent, trackable complaint mechanism.
  • Support cashless payment for subsidised goods.
  • Build a foundation ready to connect to a real backend and database.
